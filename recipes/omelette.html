Omelette

Description

Omelette is a quick and protein-rich breakfast made from beaten eggs.

It can be customized with vegetables, cheese, and spices.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 chopped green chili
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper
  • 1 tsp oil or butter

Steps

  1. Break eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add salt, pepper, onion, and chili.
  3. Beat the mixture well.
  4. Heat oil or butter in a pan.
  5. Pour the egg mixture into the pan.
  6. Cook on medium flame until golden.
  7. Flip carefully and cook the other side.
  8. Serve hot.
