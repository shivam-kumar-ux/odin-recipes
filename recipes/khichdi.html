Khichdi

Description

Khichdi is a simple and comforting Indian dish made with rice and lentils.

It is light on the stomach, easy to digest, and perfect for a healthy meal.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rice
  • 1/2 cup moong dal
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 chopped tomato
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 2 cups water

Steps

  1. Wash rice and dal properly.
  2. Heat oil or ghee in a pressure cooker.
  3. Add onion and sauté until golden.
  4. Add tomato, turmeric, and salt.
  5. Add rice and dal, then mix well.
  6. Add water and close the lid.
  7. Cook for 3 whistles and let pressure release.
  8. Serve hot with curd or pickle.
