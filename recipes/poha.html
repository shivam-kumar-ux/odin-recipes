Poha

Description

Poha is a light and healthy Indian breakfast made from flattened rice.

It is easy to cook and perfect for quick morning meals.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Poha
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1 chopped green chili
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Wash the poha lightly.
  2. Heat oil in a pan.
  3. Add onion and chili, sauté well.
  4. Add turmeric and salt.
  5. Add poha and mix gently.
  6. Cook for 5 minutes and serve hot.
